Information

Drums are an extremely old instrument dating back to around 6000BC. Of course, back then drums would have looked a lot different to what they are now however the basic philosophy is the same. Before the modern area, the Drum Kit was split up into different pieces so different people were playing a different part of the Drums. This proved ineffective later, and as musicians started to get paid more and more it was proved too expensive as well. It wasn’t until 1930 until the Drum Kit we know today was finalized on and used by the majority of musicians. Even today though there are different types of drum kit that may seem a bit different to normal.
